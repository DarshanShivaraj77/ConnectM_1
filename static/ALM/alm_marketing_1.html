Battery Warranty Intelligence

From Reactive Claims
to Data-Driven Warranty Control

Battery warranties are one of the largest and most opaque cost centers for EV OEMs, battery manufacturers, fleet operators, and financiers. ConnectM ALM transforms warranty management from a manual, dispute-driven process into a transparent, data-backed decision system.

The Problem

No Visibility Into Usage

OEMs and battery suppliers lack real-world visibility into how batteries are charged, discharged, and operated. Claims are evaluated without context.

High False & Fraudulent Claims

Deep discharge, overheating, fast-charging abuse, and poor maintenance often go undetected, leading to unwarranted claim approvals.

Slow & Manual Claim Processing

Claims rely on dealer observations and paperwork, increasing turnaround time, disputes, and customer dissatisfaction.

Rising Warranty Reserves

Without predictive insight, OEMs over-provision warranty reserves, directly impacting profitability and balance sheets.

How ConnectM Solves It

Real-Time Battery Telemetry

Continuous monitoring of SOH, SOC, temperature, charge/discharge cycles, and fault codes directly from BMS and IoT devices.

Warranty-Aware Rule Engine

Warranty terms are digitized into enforceable rules: operating thresholds, abuse conditions, and breach counters are tracked automatically.

Usage & Health-Based Scoring

Each battery is scored on health, safety, usage behavior, and maintenance compliance relative to similar assets.

Automated Claim Intelligence

Claims are supported with objective evidence: usage history, breach events, and comparative analytics, enabling faster approve / reject decisions.

Lifecycle & Policy Integration

Repeated offenders or degraded batteries are automatically flagged for quarantine, extended warranty exclusion, or decommission recommendations.

Battery Passport Linkage

Warranty history becomes part of the Battery Passport, supporting resale, second-life decisions, and regulatory audits.

Business Impact

20–40%

Reduction in warranty claim costs through fraud detection and early abuse identification.

30–50%

Faster claim turnaround time (TAT) with data-backed, automated assessments.

25–30%

Improvement in battery lifespan through proactive alerts and best-practice enforcement.

↓ Reserves

Optimized warranty provisioning using predictive health and usage analytics.

Battery Passport & ESG Intelligence

Lifecycle Transparency for Batteries
Built for Regulation & Sustainability

ConnectM ALM provides a digital Battery Passport that captures the complete lifecycle of batteries—from manufacturing to second life and decommission.

What is a Battery Passport?

A structured, verifiable digital record documenting origin, chemistry, usage, health, and end-of-life status of a battery—designed for regulatory and ESG reporting.

Manufacturing & Origin

  • Model, chemistry, capacity
  • Plant & batch traceability
  • Supplier lineage

Operational Footprint

  • Charge/discharge cycles
  • SOH degradation trends
  • Geography & duty cycles

Warranty & Safety

  • Warranty validity & claims
  • Fault & safety events
  • Abuse indicators

Second-Life & Reuse

  • Residual capacity assessment
  • Reuse/repurpose eligibility
  • Valuation intelligence

Decommission & Recycling

  • Controlled decommission workflows
  • Immutable lifecycle snapshot
  • Recycling & disposal records

ESG & Compliance

  • Scope-3 emissions inputs
  • Circular-economy reporting
  • Audit-ready evidence

Why It Matters

Battery passports are becoming mandatory across regions. ConnectM enables OEMs, fleets, financiers, and recyclers to meet ESG and regulatory requirements with verifiable data—not declarations. Transparent, verifiable lifecycle data supports sustainability, regulatory compliance, and circular-economy initiatives.
