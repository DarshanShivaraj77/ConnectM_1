Workspace & Asset Lifecycle Governance

1. Workspace Model

Every organization has mandatory system-controlled primary workspaces:

  • Central Inventory (procured, not deployed)
  • Quarantine (risk isolation)
  • Decommissioned Assets (terminal)

Primary workspaces represent physical ownership. Secondary workspaces provide logical overlays (warranty, region, cluster).

2. Asset Lifecycle

Central Inventory → Operational → Quarantine → Decommission (Terminal)

Illegal transitions are blocked at both UI and API layers.

3. SLA Governance

  • Inventory aging SLA
  • Operational uptime SLA
  • Quarantine resolution SLA
  • Decommission approval SLA

Breaches escalate automatically to compliance administrators.

4. Policy-Driven Decisions

Rule and AI engines generate recommendations, not actions. Human approval is mandatory for decommissioning.

5. Immutable Snapshot

At decommission, the platform captures a cryptographically hashed snapshot:

  • Telemetry & health
  • Warranty & claims
  • Financial exposure
  • SLA & violation history

Snapshots are append-only and audit-safe.
